# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Focus feedback on ideas and code, not people.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Trolling, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

For conduct issues, open a private security-style report as described in `SECURITY.md` and mark it as conduct-related.
